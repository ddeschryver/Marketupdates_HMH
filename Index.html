☘ EduStaff Staffing Spotlight — Week of March 17

The substitute teacher shortage is reaching a legislative tipping point. Illinois lawmakers are advancing a bill to allow retired teachers to work more substitute hours — with 87% of surveyed districts reporting teacher shortages and 91% struggling to fill substitute positions. Meanwhile, Michigan launched a new ECE teacher apprenticeship pipeline, and states from Louisiana to Colorado are advancing teacher pay legislation. These signals collectively point to a tightening labor market — and growing urgency for districts to secure reliable staffing partners like EduStaff.
